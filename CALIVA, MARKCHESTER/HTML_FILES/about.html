La Union is a province in the Northern Region of the Philippines. It is known for its culture and beautiful beaches.

Capital and Largest City: San Fernando

Founded: March 2, 1850

Highest Elevation: Mount Talalang 1,520 m

Languages: Ilocano; Pangasinan; Kankanaey; Ibaloi; Tagalog; English

ZIP code: 2500–2520

Land Area: 1,493 km²
